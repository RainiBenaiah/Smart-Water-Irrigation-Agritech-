What Do We Do?

(AgriTech) Smart Water Irrigation is a system that helps farmers to monitor and control the amount of water that is being used in their farms. This system is designed to help farmers to save water and increase the yield of their crops. The system uses sensors to monitor the soil moisture and weather conditions in the farm. The data collected by the sensors is sent to a central server where it is analyzed and used to control the irrigation system. The system can be controlled remotely using a mobile phone or a computer. The system is easy to install and can be used in all types of farms.

Smart Watering Requirements Intervention

Smart Watering requires little intervention from you, all you have to do is
